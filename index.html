About

The premier nail salon of Gainesville, Fl.

  • Creating Customer Value

    Nail1st is a premium salon and spa that is located in the heart of Gainesville, FL in the Oaks Mall just besides the Belk store. We have been associated with the beauty business for years where we have managed to formulate some of the most exquisite trends in the business of foot and hand services. With our award winning pedicure and manicure services that cover a range of features, we have become the most credited name in the beauty business across the state of Florida. At Nail1st, we have one simple ideology which we strive to maintain on a daily basis – Offer Quality under any Circumstance. This is why we do not focus on the monetary features of our work. We love our job so much that we offer all our services at the most economical rates which are formulated for the sole intention of giving our customers complete value for money.

  • Updated Techniques & Trends

    At the Nail1st Nails & Spa, our staff has been associating themselves with consistently learning and implementing new techniques to develop strategies that will remain to be on top of the beauty business. To accomplish this, we attempt to consistently learn new techniques and implementations so as to keep up with our competition. All the staff at our Gainesville, FL location has been developing newer and innovative measures so as to develop state-of-the-art procedures in offering pedicure, manicure, nail treatments, acrylics and other services. Our motive and intentions have been simple so as to maintain great customer relations while offering them the best services we have on offer. You can contact us today to make an appointment or can simply walk in our nail salon in the Oaks Mall at any time. Our doors are always open for you. We offer a clean and complete range of services that come with knowing that all your requirements are taken care of in a professional and well mannered approach.
